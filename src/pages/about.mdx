# About

![](brent-jackson.jpg)

Brent Jackson lives in Brooklyn, NY with his partner and calico roommate.
Originally from West Virginia and with a degree in Sociology from Marshall University, his career in tech has led him from UX design to front-end development.
He is currently working on themes at [Gatsby][], and is passionate about open source software.

Jackson has previously lived in Osaka, Shanghai, Washington D.C., and San Francisco,
and worked at Kickstarter, Etsy, LivingSocial, Stitch Fix, and Priceline.
He has worked on several open source projects, including [Basscss][], [CSS Stats][], [Colorable][], [Rebass][], [Styled System][], [Theme UI][], and [MDX Deck][].
